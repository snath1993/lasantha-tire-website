Tyre Care Blueprint

Sri Lanka Edition
Why this routine? Designed specifically for high-humidity conditions and the stop-and-go traffic of Colombo, ensuring maximum tyre life and safety.
WEEKLY

The Quick Check

  • Check Cold PSI Measure pressure in the morning before driving. Heat from Colombo traffic increases pressure readings falsely.
  • Tread Wear Bar Inspection Look for the small rubber bars between treads. If they are flush with the tyre surface, replace immediately.
MONTHLY

The Maintenance Log

  • Wheel Balancing Check Essential if you feel vibrations at 60-80 km/h. Potholes can knock weights off easily.
  • Rotation Log Update Plan your next rotation (every 5,000 KM). Regular rotation prevents uneven wear on FWD vehicles.
QUARTERLY

Safety & Emergency

  • Roadside Emergency Kit Verify jack functionality, wheel brace (wrench), and warning triangle are present and not rusted.
  • Spare Tyre Inspection Often forgotten! Check its pressure (usually 35-40 PSI) and ensure it holds air.
